A Comprehensive Measure of Well-Being

A Comprehensive Measure of Well-Being is a machine learning application that predicts the overall well-being score of a country or region using important indicators such as Health, Education, Income, Environment, Safety, and Social Support. The system analyzes these factors and provides an estimated well-being score, helping users understand the quality of life based on multiple dimensions instead of relying on a single indicator. This application demonstrates how Artificial Intelligence and Machine Learning can support social and economic analysis through accurate predictions.
